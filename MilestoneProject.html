Findings

  1. We notice that the most frequent words in our data set are the stopwords.
  2. The following words are most likely to be follow by “The”: of, in, to, on, at, and
  3. I is nost likely to be follow by “have”
  4. In and IS are most of the time follow by “a”
  5. and so on …. This information is very useful and will allow us to better design our prediction algorithm.
